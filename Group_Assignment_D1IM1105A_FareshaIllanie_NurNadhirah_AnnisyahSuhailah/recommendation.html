Skydiving

Skydiving is an "extreme sports". It is parachuting from plane for fun. This activity can be done by individually or with groups of people.We recommended at Skydiving Langkawi because they have safety gear that are top notch and there are many positive review that have given by the persons who have experience it.

Bungee Jumping

Bungee jumping also spelled bungy jumping, is an activity that involves a person jumping from a great height while connected to a large elastic cord.The place that you can try this activity is located at Sunway Lagoon Extreme Park.Bungy Jump at Sunway Lagoon Extreme Park is a pay-per-ride attraction. On top of the admission rate at Sunway Lagoon, the bungee jumping experience is priced at RM85 per person (Malaysians) and RM146 per person (non-Malaysians). Do note that this activity has a weight limit of minimum 40kg and maximum 250kg.

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is a mode of underwater diving whereby divers use breathing equipment that is completely independent of a surface air supply.The place that are recommended for this activity is at,lying on the Eastern coast of peninsular Malaysia, Redang Island is part of the Terengganu Marine Park and offers superb diving opportunities, with clear calm waters bejewelled with rich and beautiful corals. With its perfect diving conditions and exotic marine treasures, it is the ideal spot for scuba diving in Malaysia near Kuala Lumpur. The 4-day PADI certification course is quite affordable here, making it a great spot to learn scuba diving in Malaysia.

Caving

This is the exploration of caves usually as part of a group and led by a guide. This is an activity available to anyone aged over 7 years old and most abilities. Caving is an outdoor activity that involves walking, climbing and sometimes even abseiling. It is a challenging activity, which can push yourself outside your comfort zones, but the reward of seeing and experiencing what few people have, is worth it. Caving enables us to discover the environment in its natural formation underground, learning about cave formations, underground rivers and much more! It will take you into a hidden world of peace and tranquillity to free the mind and learn a breadth of new experiences.

    Essential Equipments:
  • Helmet with a headlamp and alternate light source
  • Knee pads
  • Boots
  • Gloves
  • Food
  • Drinking Water

Rock Climbing

Rock climbing is an activity in which participants climb up, down or across natural rock formations or artificial rock walls. The goal is to reach the summit of a formation or the endpoint of a pre-defined route without falling. Rock climbing is a physically and mentally demanding sport, one that often tests a climber’s strength, endurance, agility and balance along with mental control. It can be a dangerous sport and knowledge of proper climbing techniques and usage of specialized climbing equipment is crucial for the safe completion of routes. Because of the wide range and variety of rock formations around the world, rock climbing has been separated into several different styles and sub-disciplines.

    Types of Rock Climbing:
  • Bouldering
  • Sport climbing
  • Top roping
  • Trad Climbing

Cycling

Cycling is an enjoyable pastime that allows you to get some fresh air as you explore your neighbourhood or head off the beaten track to be in nature. But health experts recommend that cycling be more than a mere hobby; it offers numerous benefits to one’s physical and mental health. An outdoor cycling can be chosen as a method of transportation and way of getting outside and exploring nature.

    Essential Equipments:
  • Road bikes
  • Helmets
  • Cycling shoes
  • Biking gloves
  • Padded bike shorts

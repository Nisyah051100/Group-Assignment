Outdoor Activities

Outdoor recreation or outdoor activity refers to recreation done outside, most commonly in natural settings. The activities that encompass outdoor recreation vary depending on the physical environment they are being carried out in. These activities can include cycling, rock-climbing, skydiving, and even bungee-jumping — and can be completed individually or collectively.

Benefits of Outdoor Activites

In addition to helping lower blood pressure, fight obesity and improve eyesight, time spent playing in parks and other green spaces is associated with improved mental health and reduced stress. Outdoor recreation also helps support the economy and drives awareness of important conservation issues.
